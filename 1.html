Aespa

Aespa is one of the 4th Generation of K-pop girl groups, especially considered as a leader of the Generation. Produced by SM entertainment, Aespa has made remarkable discography and effect on the K-pop market in the world. They made a debut on Nov 17, 2020 with debut track 'Black Mamba'. Early from their debut, they have got a lot of expectation from K-pop fans and also fans of SM entertainment. This is because their ancestors had made great footprint on K-pop market: SNSD, F(x) and Red Velvet.

These members have their specialty and originality at their musical performance. Also, they are famous for the completion of their universe; represented by ae, naevis, Gwang-ya(Wilderness). This universe has been getting deeper and deeper within their title tracks 'Next Level' and 'Savage'. With their outstanding performance and perfection in their universe, they have the biggest fandom among all groups of the 4th Generation of K-pop girl groups.
