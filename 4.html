Fromis_9

Fromis_9 is one of the late runner who came in the 4th Generation, since their debut is on Jan 24, 2018 with debut album 'Glass Shoes'. They made a debut in Stone Music entertainment at first, but now they are in PLEDIS entertainment, which is subordinated in HYBE entertainment. The group first had produced by idol audition program called 'idol school', so their belong was once to the Off The Record Entertainment, which belongs to CJ E&M. They haven't made success until their 4th title track 'Love Bomb', but with their effort and growth on musical abilities and concepts, they made first won in chart with their most recent track 'Talk n Talk'.

Their biggest potential is in their individual ability to make fans. Since they weren't trainee in such entertainments, nine different colors have could combined diversely so that they can make plenty of concepts fit well to themselves. They also have strength on making their concepts reflecting current issues. One of their latest albums '9-way Ticket' is based on quarantine via COVID-19. Its MV show their visuals with online presets, so that people can feel sympathy and kind of relief by the music and concept they've made.
