STAYC

STAYC is one of the 4th Generation of K-pop girl groups, produced by HighUp Entertainment, known as an entertainment established by BEP. BEP was a producer of Twice when they were at their career-high from 2015 to 2017, who made a No.1 on the annual chart at Melon with 'Cheer Up' of Twice. Considering this, you can feel the pieces of Twice in STAYC's tracks. They made a debut on Nov 12, 2020 with their debut track 'SO BAD'. The title of their track is one of their specialty, which is all upper-case so that it looks like highlighted or something.

The strongest and biggest potential of STAYC is their style of vocals. Nowadays, most vocal styles of K-pop groups are heading to the mixture of autotunes and rich beats so that vocals are considered as one of the components in a track. However, the style of vocal that STAYC has almost follow their ancestors; especially 2nd Generation such as SNSD, Wonder Girls, etc. Their dry, full vocal is fully enough to make listeners feel a kind of nostalgia at old-time tracks of K-pop.
